About EcoMission

EcoMission is a community-driven platform that connects individuals who care about the environment. Whether it’s a polluted pond, an overflowing trash site, or any local environmental issue — you can report it, and together with local volunteers, organize clean-up events to make a real impact.

EcoMission empowers citizens to take action. Instead of waiting for change, let's build it together — one cleanup at a time.
